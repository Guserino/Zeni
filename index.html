Zeni

Blockchain | DeFi project by GVA

About

The ZENI, named after Earth's currency in DragonBall Z, is the digital token that powers the Zeni blockchain.
Zeni is a simple proof of stake blockchain that hosts a 'virtual/fake' DeFi ecosystem. Current Project:

  • Zeni | buildout of blockchain infrastructure
  • Toki | Zeni's Decentralized Exchange (DEX)
