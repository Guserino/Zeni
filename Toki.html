Toki DEX

Marketplace to buy and sell tokens and earn yield on investments. Toki deploys an AMM to create liquidity markets for tokens on the Zeni chain. Unlike traditional markets, in which liquidity is provided via order books, Toki's AMM protocol allows market participants to swap tokens and interact directly with its liquidity pools
